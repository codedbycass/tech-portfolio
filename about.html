Learn more about me

As a child of Lao refugees, I didn’t have a blueprint written out for me about how to navigate success in the US. At first, the ambiguity and lack of direction scared me, but I took it as an invitation to nurture my curiosities and freedoms.

As a software engineer, I aim to combine my technical knowledge and diverse experiences–– customer service working with people and databases; academic training in the sciences and humanities honing my critical thinking and diligence; project management experience developing and leading a culturally relevant digital equity program for immigrants and refugees–– to make technologies that serve, not only the average user, but those who are socially and economically marginalized from meaningfully participating in both the digital and material world.
