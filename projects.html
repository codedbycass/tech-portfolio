Go With the Flow (2023)

Go with the Flow is a comprehensive full-stack application that serves as a versatile tool for tracking menstrual cycles and overall sexual health and wellness to be used on and off the cycle. Users initiate entries by entering the date and selecting their period status. By logging daily and gathering scientific data on themselves, users gain insights into their behaviors and patterns throughout their cycles. This app prioritizes security, requiring user login to safeguard sensitive health information. Data is not shared with anyone.

Technologies: Node.js, JavaScript, EJS, Bootstrap CSS
